Supported Formats

Eternal Export Format

Copy a deck from Eternal or EternalWarcry: 

4 Torch (Set1 #8)
4 Oni Ronin (Set1 #13)
4 Champion of Fury (Set2 #187)
4 Fire Sigil (Set1 #1)
---------MARKET---------
1 Bore (Set1003 #1)

Simple Format

Just card names and quantities: 

4 Torch
4 Oni Ronin
4 Champion of Fury
4 Fire Sigil
MARKET
1 Bore

Tips

  • - Include set numbers for best matching accuracy
  • - Use "MARKET" or dashes to separate market cards
  • - Card names are matched case-insensitively
  • - Lines starting with # are ignored (comments)

How to Export from EternalWarcry

  1. 1. Go to a deck page on EternalWarcry
  2. 2. Click "Export" button
  3. 3. Select "Eternal Format" or "Text"
  4. 4. Copy the deck list
  5. 5. Paste it here
